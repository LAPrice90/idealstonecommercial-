Ideal Stone Commercial

Ideal Stone Commercial, a division of Ideal Group, is committed to delivering premium porcelain solutions tailored specifically for the commercial sector across the UK and Ireland. With a focus on excellence in both form and function, we specialise in fabricating bespoke surfaces for hospitality, retail, office, and large-scale residential developments. Our worktops, tabletops, and cladding solutions are crafted to meet the rigorous demands of commercial environments, combining cutting-edge aesthetics with long-term performance.

Operating from our advanced production facility in County Monaghan, we use state-of-the-art machinery and precision engineering to manufacture custom pieces from industry-leading materials such as Laminam porcelain. Our expert team collaborates closely with architects, designers, and contractors to deliver turnkey surface solutions that meet the exacting standards of modern commercial interiors and exteriors. Every project is approached with a commitment to detail, efficiency, and design integrity.

Ideal Stone Commercial builds on the strong foundation of Ideal Group’s longstanding reputation for quality, innovation, and reliability. As we continue to expand our commercial portfolio, we remain dedicated to setting new benchmarks in surface design and installation. Whether for hotels, restaurants, offices, or high-end retail, we provide elegant, durable, and low-maintenance porcelain solutions that elevate the spaces they serve.
